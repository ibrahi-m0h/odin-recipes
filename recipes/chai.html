Chai Recipe

Description

This is a cup of chai. Chai is where it's at. You can make it many ways but desi way is the best way to make it.

Ingredients

  • Tapal Black tea bag
  • Carnation Evaporated Milk
  • Ilachi
  • One Packet Splenda

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add tea bag
  3. Simmer for 7 minutes
  4. Add carnation
  5. Add splenda
  6. Mix and serve
