Smoothie Recipe

Description

This is a fruit smoothie that has protein to help you reach your macros!

Ingredients

  • Frozen strawberries
  • Oatmilk
  • Vanilla Protein powder
  • Splenda

Steps

  1. Add strawberries to blender
  2. Add milk
  3. Blend until uniform
  4. Add protein powder
  5. Add splenda
  6. Blend and serve
